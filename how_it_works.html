How it works

Step 1

Client fills out the required fields, departure point, arrival point, date, time, no of people, round trip, one way or multi leg.

Step 2

Athemis returns results in ascending price order.

Step 3

Client picks chooses desired flights they want.

Step 4

Client either logs into account if they have one or create one if new user.

Step 5

Once in account area client confirms desired charter once more.

Step 6

Client is taken to page stage for payment.

Step 7

Payment is taken and client will be sent a confirmation email.

Step 8

Client logs of the system.

Step 9

Client is updated with email up to flight date.
