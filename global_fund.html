Global fund

Athemis Aviation, in its commitment to corporate integrity and responsibility will be organizing global fundraising events to bring together the influencers of today’s industry to create one voice for human compassion. Athemis Aviation will working with international events management companies, concert promoters and media companies to raise funds to benefit humanitarian work, environmental disasters, health aids and more across the globe.

In addition, we will be organizing a series of Mind Summits, bringing together the thinkers of our time to address the changing world. We would like to look at countries where technology, food production, education and global business can help improve the everyday lives of the people living in those countries.

In discussion with the Fortune 500 and leading large “small” business companies, financial sponsorship is warranted for such ongoing events.

The Athemis Aviation Global Fund

In its commitment to corporate integrity and responsibility will be starting a global fund to aid causes around the world such as humanitarian, environmental disasters, health aids etc. We believe we can give money to a charity but too many charities have been in the news recently and not for the right reasons but for all the wrong reasons. We have a chosen few which we will be working with but also we will be doing a lot of work ourselves. Gavin Nathan Founder and Chairman; wants to see the money actually being spend where he can see the results and impact immediately wherever that need may be. Even though Athemis Aviation is a newbie in the aviation scene we have a strict policy and a rule that for every hour that we work and make money we put money into this fund for the mentioned purpose.

We strongly believe in giving back from day one or as we like to quote first flight, first hour instead of waiting to the end of the year to give something back. Gavin Nathan stands by this strongly and wants to change the way people give money and how its receive by the end parties. He have sat and looked on for way to long and have seen a lot done but also very little being done which made him come up with this concept. Far too many are not being reach with the monies being raised so why not do something I love and make a difference while doing that from first flight, first hour. Bill Gates once said

“Is the rich world aware of how four of the six billion live? If we were aware, we would want to help, we’d want to get involved”

Gavin Nathan aim is to grow this fund worldwide and turn it into a real game changer. The way we see this is we can either give the money to a charity or we can use the money directly ourselves to go and purchase the relevant supplies such as medicines, tents, food, and much more allowing us to see the results of our hard work and money having an impact straight away.

We aim to raise money from our own revenue inwards into the company, every jet every hour $$$ goes into the fund and by organising global fundraising events to bring together the influencers of today’s industry to create one voice for human compassion. Athemis Aviation wants to work with international events management companies, concert promoters and media companies, PR companies to raise funds to benefit our fund and then in return benefit humanitarian work, environmental disasters, health aids and more causes across the globe.

In addition, we would like to produce a series of Mind Summits, bringing together the thinkers of our time to address the changing world. We would like to look at countries where technology, food production, education and global business can help improve the everyday lives of the people living in those countries.

I believe that you can help us with this as you have a very good network and we would like to spread the word globally to get as much support as needed to grow this fund into a success.

Gavin Nathan Founder and President
