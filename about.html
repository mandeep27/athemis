About Us/Team

Welcome to Athemis Aviation

Founded and driven by enthusiast in technology, aviation, and business, Athemis Jets is bringing a new perspective on innovation into the industry. With a diverse team of individuals from around the globe we have built and continue to engineer the most powerful charter booking platform to meet the challenges of modern private jet charter users.

Our approach, very simple; to deliver the highest quality system and customer satisfaction a client can have. In other words we treat our clients as we would like to be treated. We fly a broad range of assignments including business, leisure.

Why use Athemis Aviation

Simply because we care; Athemis Jets steps into the new wave of aviation entrepreneurship with social responsible beneficence at the core of the business. The Athemis Jets mantra of giving back (Every Jet, Every Hour in flight benefits global causes) from day one as we conduct business is the right thing to do. We support various global causes but have a special focus on food, water and humanitarian relief efforts. We want to create a network of business minded benefactors who see charitable generosity not as an option but as a responsibility to the world..

Famous Quote; Warren Buffett contends that a lifetime of wealth building that it’s time “give back”; Mark Zuckerberg, as a young billionaire, says the advantage of accumulating wealth at a young age is to see the results of your philanthropy immediately.

Athemis believes “giving back”, is a beneficent act parallel to earnings—donate as you earn.
